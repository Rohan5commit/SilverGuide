# SilverGuide Demo Script

## 2 to 3 minute flow

### 0:00 to 0:20

Open the homepage.

Say:

"This is SilverGuide, a senior-friendly web coach built for GenLink Hacks. It helps seniors practice everyday tech skills, spot scams, and learn safe AI habits in a calm, readable format."

### 0:20 to 1:00

Open `Scam Shield`.

Show the fake bank text example.

Ask:

"Would you trust this message?"

Click `Very suspicious`.

Show the red flags and say:

"SilverGuide explains the risk in plain language: urgency, an unfamiliar link, and pressure to act fast. It also teaches what never to do, like sharing one-time passcodes."

### 1:00 to 1:35

Open `AI Practice`.

Show the built-in `Using ChatGPT and AI tools safely` lesson.

Then show the `Prompt Coach`.

Use the sample prompt about writing a polite message to a doctor.

Say:

"SilverGuide uses NVIDIA NIM to make prompts clearer and safer, while reminding users not to include private personal details and to verify important advice."

### 1:35 to 2:10

Open `Everyday Tech Coach`.

Choose `Joining a video call`.

Click through one or two steps.

Optionally press `Listen to this step`.

Say:

"Every lesson is one step at a time, with large buttons, readable text, and optional voice playback. There is also a simpler reading version for accessibility."

### 2:10 to 2:35

Open `Practice Mode`.

Answer one question.

Say:

"Practice Mode reinforces habits without feeling like a stressful test. Feedback is gentle, and each session ends with a simple summary of what the learner practiced and what to remember."

### 2:35 to 3:00

Close on the module overview or session summary.

Say:

"This is something a senior center could use immediately. It runs in the browser, needs no login for the MVP, includes built-in content, and uses AI only where it truly helps learners feel safer and more confident."
